┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ DATABASE SCHEMA                                                             │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

sft_ranked_data table (NEW COLUMNS):

  domain              TEXT            Medical department
  sme_score           INTEGER (1-5)   Expert quality rating
  sme_score_reason    TEXT            Expert's reasoning
  sme_reviewed_by     TEXT            Reviewer name
  sme_reviewed_at     TIMESTAMP       Review timestamp

INDEXES:
  • idx_sft_ranked_data_domain (domain)
  • idx_sft_ranked_data_sme_score (sme_score)
